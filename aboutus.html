ABOUT US

ATHENE is a ed-tech company. Launched in 2020, ATHENE offers an effective exam conducting sourse for various colleges and schools to enable examination from home for students. ATHENE has expanded the availability of at home testing for any academic or general examinations to everywhere. ATHENE has provided for students who have been affected by the coronavirus (COVID-19). Monitoring of a test is conducted using live remote proctors and artificial intelligence technology. The tests are identical in content, format, on-screen experience and scoring to the normal computer based online tests at a test center. It is monitored online by a trained human proctor from start to finish to maintain test security.
